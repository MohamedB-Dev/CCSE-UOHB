Welcome to College of Computer Science and Engineering

The College of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Hafar Al-Batin was established in the academic year 2014/2015, to produce qualified students in the field of computers.


The college awards bachelor's degrees in four different majors:
  • Computer Science and Engineering
  • Software Engineering
  • Cyber Security
  • Data science
    • Vision
    Leadership in education and scientific research in the fields of computing and knowledge technology
    Mission
    Contribute to the transfer of our society to the level of modern knowledge. Through innovative teaching and practical research in computing technology, knowledge that develops creative analytical and methodological capabilities
    Objectives
    • Keep up with the study plans according to the best international standards
    • Creating a spirit of competition and creativity among graduates and consolidating this principle and qualifying them for that
    • Providing advanced infrastructure and services
    • Forming partnerships with the community
    • Providing modern academic programs that meet international standards and meet market needs
